Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our design process, project timeline, and what to expect when working with us.

What happens after the design is ready & I approve it?

How long does the development process take?

The development timeline varies based on project complexity. Typically, it takes between 2-4 weeks after design approval. We'll provide a detailed timeline after reviewing your approved designs.

Will you handle the website deployment?

Yes, we handle all aspects of deployment. We'll ensure your website is properly set up on your hosting platform, perform thorough testing, and make sure everything is working perfectly before going live.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Absolutely! We offer 30 days of complimentary support after launch to address any issues. After that, we have various maintenance plans you can choose from to ensure your website continues to perform optimally.

Can I make changes after approval?

Minor changes are possible, but significant alterations after approval may incur additional costs and delay the timeline. We encourage thorough review during the design phase to avoid this situation.

What if I need additional features later?

We're happy to help with future enhancements! We can discuss additional features and provide a separate quote for implementing them after your website is live.
